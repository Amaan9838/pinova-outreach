## 🎯 TARGET SEGMENTS (Priority Order)

### 1. **High-Volume Teams (5-20 agents)** - HIGHEST ROI
- Pain: Drowning in leads, can't follow up fast enough
- Budget: $500-2000/month
- Decision maker: Team leader/broker
- Close rate: 15-20%

### 2. **Solo Top Producers ($5M+ annual)** - EASIEST CLOSE
- Pain: Want to scale but can't hire fast enough
- Budget: $300-800/month
- Decision maker: The agent themselves
- Close rate: 25-30%

### 3. **Brokerages (20+ agents)** - BIGGEST DEALS
- Pain: Agents not using their current CRM
- Budget: $2000-10000/month
- Decision maker: Broker/owner + tech committee
- Close rate: 5-10% (long sales cycle)

## 💎 GOLDEN RULES

1. **Lead with THEIR pain, not YOUR product**
   - Bad: "We have AI CRM software"
   - Good: "You're losing 40% of your paid leads"

2. **Use specific numbers**
   - Bad: "Increase conversions"
   - Good: "Book 12 extra appointments per month"

3. **Name drop competitors**
   - "3 other teams in [City] are already using this"
   - Creates FOMO

4. **Always offer a trial**
   - Removes risk
   - Gets them using it (sticky)

5. **Follow up 7-12 times**
   - Most agents are busy
   - Persistence wins
